FAQ

You can ask your question...

How do I use this habit tracker? >
Start by adding the habits you want to track. Check them off daily as you complete them, and watch your streaks and progress grow!
Is my data private and secure? >
Yes, your data is saved automatically if you’re logged in. If you’re using the app offline, it will sync when you reconnect to the internet.
Is my data private and secure? >
Yes, your data is encrypted and stored securely. We take your privacy seriously and do not share your information with third parties.
What kinds of habits can I track? >
You can track any habit you want, such as exercising, reading, drinking water, meditating, or spending less time on social media.
Can I export my data? >
Yes, you can download your habit data as a CSV file to analyze or keep for personal records.
